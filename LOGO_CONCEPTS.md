# PrimeHaul OS - Logo Concept Designs

## Design Brief

**Brand Values:** Professional | Friendly | Efficient | Trustworthy  
**Primary Color:** Prime Green (#2EE59D)  
**Secondary Color:** Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1C)  
**Accent Color:** Accent Blue (#0066FF)

**Key Concepts to Express:**
- Speed & Efficiency (instant quotes)
- Movement & Transportation (removals)
- Technology & AI (smart automation)
- Professional yet approachable

---

## Logo Variations

### Primary Logo (Full)
- Logo mark + wordmark "PrimeHaul OS"
- Horizontal layout
- Prime Green logo, Deep Charcoal text

### Secondary Logo (Stacked)
- Logo mark above wordmark
- Vertical layout
- For square spaces

### Icon Only
- Just the logo mark
- For favicons, app icons, social media
- Minimum size: 32px

### Monochrome
- Single color version
- For single-color printing
- Deep Charcoal or white

### Reversed
- White logo on dark backgrounds
- For dark themes

---

## Typography Pairing

**Logo Wordmark:** Bold, modern sans-serif
- Weight: 700 (Bold)
- Letter spacing: -0.5px (tighter)
- Style: Uppercase or Title Case

**Tagline:** Light, friendly sans-serif
- Weight: 300-400 (Light-Regular)
- Letter spacing: Normal
- Style: Sentence case

---

## Color Applications

### Light Background
- Logo: Prime Green (#2EE59D)
- Text: Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1C)

### Dark Background
- Logo: Prime Green (#2EE59D) or White
- Text: White or Light Gray (#F5F5F7)

### Single Color
- Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1C) or White
- For printing, embroidery, etc.

---

## Size Guidelines

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Full logo: 120px width
- Icon only: 32px × 32px
- Wordmark only: 100px width

**Recommended Sizes:**
- Website header: 180px width
- Business card: 60mm width
- App icon: 1024px × 1024px (iOS), 512px × 512px (Android)

---

## Usage Guidelines

**DO:**
- Maintain clear space (2x logo height minimum)
- Use approved colors only
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Use high-resolution versions

**DON'T:**
- Stretch or distort logo
- Rotate logo
- Change colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients) without approval
- Place on busy backgrounds without clear space
